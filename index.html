TUNISIA

Tunisia,officially the Republic of Tunisia

Tunisia is a North African country located on the Mediterranean coast and bordering the Sahara Desert. The Bardo National Museum in Tunis, the capital, exhibits archaeological finds ranging from Roman mosaics to Islamic art. The Medina district, with its bustling souk, encircles the imposing Zitouna Mosque. To the east, on the site of the ancient city of Carthage, are the Antonine Baths and other ruins, and the National Museum of Carthage and its numerous artifacts.

Capital: Tunis
President: Kaïs Saïed
Official language: Arabic
Motto: in Arabic: حرية، نظام، عدالة (Ḥoṛiya, Niẓam, 'Adāla, "Liberty, Order, Justice")
